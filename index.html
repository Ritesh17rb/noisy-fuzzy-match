Matching Settings Guide
Fuzzy Ratios
  • token_sort_ratio — best when words are the same but order differs.
    Example: “Smith John” ↔ “John Smith”
  • token_set_ratio — ignores extra words; good for companies with suffixes.
    Example: “Google LLC” ↔ “Google Alphabet”
  • ratio — simple typo-tolerant match.
    Example: “Jon” ↔ “John”
    • Threshold (0–100)
    • ≥ 80 — strict, high confidence
    • 60–79 — balanced default
    • < 60 — permissive, may require review
    Quick Tips
    • Use token_sort_ratio for human names.
    • Use token_set_ratio for company variations.
    • Increase threshold if matches feel noisy.
    • Use manual locks to force correct pairs.
